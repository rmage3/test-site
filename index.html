Hello

I’m based in the UK, in the South-East of England. I am an electronic musician, a media and website designer, a drummer and teacher, and a digital artist.

My Skills

Designer

I design in a modern and minimalist way, putting my own spin on a gothic style. I try to make my design appealing and useable, while also creating a striking and unique visual style.

Coder

I have just started learning to develop for the web - this is the second website I've coded. My first was purely in HTML, whereas this one uses CSS too. I've designed websites on Wix before, but never like this. I'm looking forward to doing more!

Drummer

I have been drumming for about 18 years, and teaching for about 3 months at a secondary school. I am planning to expand my student base at this and other schools, and eventually set up my own drum teaching studio.

Composer

I compose and produce in FL Studio, although I also occasionally use Cubase or Logic. I experiment with different sonic textures and rhythmic elements to create dark alternative electronic music.
